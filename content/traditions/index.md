# Traditions

The intellectual and spiritual lineages that feed into the Relational Frontier's project.

## Core Traditions

- [[relational-psychoanalysis|Relational Psychoanalysis]] — The clinical tradition mapping the relational constitution of selfhood
- [[phenomenology|Phenomenology]] — The philosophical study of lived experience from the inside
- [[imaginal-tradition|The Imaginal Tradition]] — The lineage of thinkers who took imagination seriously as an organ of perception
- [[existentialism|Existentialism]] — The philosophical tradition of the concrete, finite, anxious, free human being

## Expanded Traditions

- [[neoplatonism|Neoplatonism]] — The ancient metaphysical tradition of participation, procession, and return; the philosophical backbone of the imaginal tradition
- [[romanticism|Romanticism]] — The literary and artistic movement defending the imagination against Enlightenment reduction; first response to the meaning crisis
- [[jungian-archetypal-psychology|Jungian and Archetypal Psychology]] — The depth-psychological tradition of archetypes, individuation, and soul-making
