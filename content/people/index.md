# People

The thinkers, writers, and practitioners whose work shapes the Relational Frontier's intellectual project.

## Philosophers & Theorists

- [[martin-buber|Martin Buber]] — The philosopher of genuine encounter and relational ontology
- [[hartmut-rosa|Hartmut Rosa]] — The sociologist of resonance and alienation
- [[john-vervaeke|John Vervaeke]] — The cognitive scientist mapping the meaning crisis
- [[henry-corbin|Henry Corbin]] — The philosopher who recovered the imaginal world
- [[emmanuel-levinas|Emmanuel Levinas]] — Ethics as first philosophy; the face of the other as absolute summons
- [[martin-heidegger|Martin Heidegger]] — Being-in-the-world, thrownness, authenticity
- [[paul-tillich|Paul Tillich]] — The Ground of Being; the courage to be; existential theology
- [[james-filler|James Filler]] — Neoplatonism, Heidegger, and relation as ontological ground
- [[maurice-merleau-ponty|Maurice Merleau-Ponty]] — The body as the vehicle of being; the flesh of the world

- [[jessica-benjamin|Jessica Benjamin]] — Mutual recognition and the intersubjective turn
- [[thomas-ogden|Thomas Ogden]] — The analytic third; psychoanalysis as art form
- [[wilfred-bion|Wilfred Bion]] — Containment, reverie, and the transformation of experience
- [[melanie-klein|Melanie Klein]] — Object relations, positions, and projective identification
- [[carl-jung|Carl Jung]] — The collective unconscious, archetypes, and individuation

- [[rollo-may|Rollo May]] — Existential psychology, anxiety, and the daimonic
- [[ernest-becker|Ernest Becker]] — The denial of death and immortality projects
