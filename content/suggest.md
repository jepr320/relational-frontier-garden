# Suggest a Node

The Relational Frontier is a living garden. It grows through genuine inquiry — and that inquiry does not stop at the current edges.

If you've encountered a thinker, concept, practice, tradition, work of art, or piece of music that you believe belongs here — something that genuinely illuminates the territory of relational depth, meaning, and the human encounter with reality — this is where to say so.

This is not a feedback form. It's an invitation to *discernment*: what makes something belong? What thread connects it to the questions this garden is trying to hold? A sentence or two of genuine reasoning will do more than a long rationale.

Suggestions are reviewed by the gardener. Not everything submitted will be added, but everything submitted will be read.
